About me

I manage the day-to-day running of ‘The Shed’, an open access workshop which provides a rapid prototyping facility for the School of Computing and the University of Kent.

My skills include: rapid prototyping and design, conducting qualitative research, user-centered design, programming in C and developing ‘Internet of Things’ applications.
